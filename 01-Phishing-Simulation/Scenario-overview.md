## Phishing Simulation Scenario

Scenario Name: Job Application Shortlisted

This scenario simulates a common phishing technique where attackers impersonate recruitment teams to exploit job seekers' urgency and curiosity.

Objective:
- Measure user interaction
- Capture realistic email headers
- Demonstrate phishing workflow safely
