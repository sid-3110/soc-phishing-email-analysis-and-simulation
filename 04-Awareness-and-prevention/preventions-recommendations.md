# Prevention & Mitigation Recommendations

## Objective
To recommend technical and awareness-based controls to reduce phishing risk based on the findings of this investigation.

---

## Technical Recommendations

### Enforce Email Authentication
- Implement and enforce SPF, DKIM, and DMARC policies
- Configure DMARC with monitoring and enforcement (quarantine/reject)

### Improve Email Filtering
- Enhance phishing detection rules
- Flag job-themed and urgent emails for additional scrutiny

### Monitoring & Logging
- Monitor repeated user interaction with suspicious emails
- Track click behavior for awareness metrics

---

## User Awareness Recommendations

### Awareness Training
- Conduct regular phishing awareness sessions
- Educate users on identifying urgency and authority-based lures

### Simulated Phishing Campaigns
- Run periodic phishing simulations
- Use results to measure awareness improvement over time

---

## SOC Process Improvements
- Standardize phishing investigation playbooks
- Include gray-area escalation paths
- Improve collaboration between SOC and awareness teams

---

## Final Note
Effective phishing prevention requires a combination of technical controls, user awareness, and consistent SOC processes. No single control is sufficient on its own.
